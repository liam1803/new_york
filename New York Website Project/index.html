New York: The Ultimate Travel Destination

Home to over 8 million people, New York has more people living here per square mile than anywhere else in America. In New York, there are many attractions and landmarks to enjoy, which is one of the many reasons why it attracts so many visitors each year. From the dizzing heights of the Empire State Building, to the more calmer and beautiful enviornment that is Central Park, there are almost an endless amount of things you can get up to. So come on, let's go and explore together.
